Visa requirements for Taiwanese citizens

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search
Taiwan passport

Visa requirements for Taiwan citizens are visa requirements for those traveling on a Taiwan passport. Taiwan passport holders can be divided into those Republic of China nationals who hold Household registration, and thus right of abode in Taiwan.,[1] (i.e. "registered nationals") and those who do not ("unregistered nationals"). In the case they hold the household registration, they are entitled to a ROC ID Card[disambiguation needed] and the number of that card is printed in the passport. Several countries and territories have more favorable visa regimes for those holding passports with this identity card number. Unregistered nationals who use the Taiwan passport to enter Taiwan require an entry permit and are subject to immigration controls. In this article the visa requirements for Taiwan passports containing an identity card number are discussed.

Currently, the Henley Visa Restrictions Index 2014 states that holders of a Taiwanese passport can travel to 132 countries visa-free/with visa on arrival. However, the Taiwan government has given an official tally of 141 countries and territories that grant visa-free or visa-on-arrival privileges to its citizens [5]. Nevertheless, gathering information from varied sources, the list below contains around 157 entries.

Vaccination[edit]

Many African countries, including Angola, Benin, Burkina Faso, Cameroon, Central African Republic, Chad, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Republic of the Congo, Côte d'Ivoire, Equatorial Guinea, Gabon, Ghana, Guinea, Liberia, Mali, Mauritania, Niger, Rwanda, São Tomé and Príncipe, Senegal, Sierra Leone, Uganda, Zambia require all incoming passengers to have a current International Certificate of Vaccination. Some other countries require vaccination only if the passenger is coming from an infected area.[23]

Passport validity[edit]

Many countries require passport validity of no less than 6 months and one or two blank pages.[24]
