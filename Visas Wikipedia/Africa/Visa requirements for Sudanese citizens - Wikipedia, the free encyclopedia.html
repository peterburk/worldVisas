Visa requirements for Sudanese citizens

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

Visa requirements for Sudanese citizens are administrative entry restrictions imposed by the authorities of other states on citizens of Sudan.

Visa free and Visa on arrival travel[edit]

The following countries allow visa free or visa on arrival access to holders of ordinary Sudanese passports :

Africa[edit]

Countries and Territories Conditions of access
 Burundi Visa issued on arrival [1]
 Cape Verde Visa issued on arrival.[2]
 Comoros A free 24 h transit visa issued upon arrival at the airport. Within 24 hours this must be converted into a full visa at the immigration office in Moroni

(fee payable)[3]
 Djibouti 1-month visa issued upon arrival for DJF5,000 [4]
 Egypt 3 month(for women, children below 16, males 50 or above)[5]
 Eritrea Visa on arrival 1 month [6]
 Gambia visa issued on arrival [7]
 Guinea-Bissau 3 month visa issued on arrival for EUR 85 [8]
 Madagascar 90-day visa issued on arrival for MGA140,000 [9]
 Mali 1 month[10]
 Mauritania 30-day visa issued on arrival [11]
 Mozambique 30-day visa issued on arrival for US$25 [12]
 Seychelles 1 month[13]
 Somalia 1 month[14]
 Togo 7-days visa issued on arrival for XOF35,000 [15]
 Uganda 3 month visa issued on arrival for US$50 [16]
 Zambia 3 month visa issued on arrival for US$50 [17]

Americas[edit]

Countries and Territories Conditions of access
 Dominica 21 days [18]
 Ecuador 90 days [19]
 Haiti 3 months [20]
 Nicaragua 90 day tourist card issued on arrival for $5 [21]
 Saint Kitts and Nevis 14 days [22]
 Saint Vincent and the Grenadines 1 month [23]

Asia[edit]

Countries and Territories Conditions of access
 Bangladesh visa on arrival for 30 days [24]
 Cambodia Visa on arrival travel [25]
 Lebanon 30 days [26]
 Laos 30-day visa issued on arrival for US$30 [27]
 Macau 30 day visa issued on arrival for 100 MOP [28]
 Malaysia 30 days [29]
 Maldives Visa on arrival travel for 30 days [30]
   Nepal 15/30/90 day visa issued upon arrival for $25/50/100 [31]
 Sri Lanka 30 days (ETA required. cost: US$ 30) [32]
 Timor-Leste 30-day visa issued upon arrival for US$30 [33]

Oceania[edit]

Countries and Territories Conditions of access
 Cook Islands 31 days [34]
 F.S. Micronesia 30 days [35]
 Niue 30 days [36]
 Palau 30 days [37]
 Samoa 60 days [38]
 Tuvalu 1 month [39]
