Visa-free and visa-on-arrival access[edit]

Visa requirements for Maldivian citizens are as follows:

Africa[edit]

  •  Algeria
  •  Botswana
  •  Burundi length of stay undetermined (VOA) - US$40.00
  •  Cape Verde (VOA)
  •  Comoros (VOA)
  •  Djibouti 1 month (VOA)
  •  Egypt 80 days (VOA)
  •  Gambia 90 days
  •  Guinea-Bissau 90 days (VOA) - XOF 55,000 [1]
  •  Kenya 3 months (VOA) - US$50.00
  •  Lesotho 14 days
  •  Madagascar 90 days (VOA)
  •  Malawi 90 days
  •  Mali [2]
  •  Mauritius 60 days
  •  Mozambique 30 days (VOA)
  •  Seychelles 1 month
  •  Somalia 30 days (VOA) - US$50 [3]
  •  South Africa 30 days
  •  Swaziland 30 days
  •  Tanzania 3 months (VOA) - US$50
  •  Togo 7 days (VOA)
  •  Tunisia 3 months
  •  Uganda 3 months (VOA) - US$50
  •  Zambia 90 days
  •  Zimbabwe 3 months

Americas[edit]

  •  Anguilla 3 months [4]
  •  Antigua and Barbuda 1 month
  •  Bahamas 3 months [5]
  •  Barbados 6 months [6]
  •  Belize 1 month [7]
  •  Bermuda 6 months
  •  Bolivia 90 days for USD 52 [8]
  •  British Virgin Islands 30 days
  •  Cayman Islands 30 days
  •  Costa Rica 30 days
  •  Dominica 21 days
  •  Ecuador 90 days [9]
  •  Grenada 3 months [10]
  •  Haiti 3 months
  •  Montserrat 3 months
  •  Panama 180 days [11]
  •  Nicaragua 90 days (VOA) - USD 20 [12]
  •  Saint Kitts and Nevis 3 months [13]
  •  Saint Lucia 6 weeks [14]
  •  Saint Vincent and the Grenadines 1 months
  •  Trinidad and Tobago 90 days
  •  Turks and Caicos Islands 30 days

Asia[edit]

  •  Armenia 120 days (VOA)
  •  Brunei 14 days [15]
  •  Cambodia 30 days (VOA)
  •  East Timor 30 days (VOA)- USD 30.00
  •  Hong Kong 3 months [16]
  •  India 3 months
  •  Indonesia 30 days (VOA) [17]
  •  Jordan 1 month (VOA)
  •  Laos 30 days (VOA)
  •  Macau 30 days (VOA)
  •  Malaysia 1 month [18]
  •    Nepal 60 days (VOA)
  •  Pakistan 3 months
  •  Philippines 21 days [19]
  •  Singapore 30 days
  •  Sri Lanka 30 days (VOA) [20]
  •  Thailand 15 days (VOA)
  •  Turkey 3 months (VOA)

Europe[edit]

  •  Ireland 3 months
  •  Kosovo 90 days
  •  United Kingdom 6 months
    •  Gibraltar
    •  Guernsey
    •  Isle of Man
    •  Jersey

Oceania[edit]

  •  Cook Islands 30 days
  •  Fiji 6 months
  •  Federated States of Micronesia 30 days
  •  Nauru 30 days (VOA)
  •  Niue 30 days
  •  Palau 30 days
  •  Papua New Guinea 60 days (VOA)
  •  Samoa 60 days
  •  Solomon Islands 3 months
  •  Tuvalu 1 month
  •  Vanuatu 30 days [21]
