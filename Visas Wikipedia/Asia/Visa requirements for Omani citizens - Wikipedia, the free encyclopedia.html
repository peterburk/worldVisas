Visa requirements for Omani citizens

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

Visa requirements for Omani citizens are administrative entry restrictions imposed by the authorities of other states on citizens of Oman. Omani citizens do not need a visa to enter other member states of the GCC. They also have the right to take up jobs in those countries. Similarly, citizens of other GCC states do not need a visa to enter Oman. Also, Omani citizens do not need a visa to enter many member states of the Arab League.

According to the "Henley Visa Restrictions Index 2012", holders of an Omani passport can visit 57 countries visa-free or with visa on arrival.[1]
